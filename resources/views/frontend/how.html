Order & Set-up

Select photo packages:
  • Pick types from drop down choices and select from additional options.

Confirm Shoot details & Pay:
  • Client will confirm the shoot: Location, date/time, package and other expected deliverables.
  • Client will provide payment authorization.
  • 100% refund for any cancelations that are more than 48 hours in advance of shoot.

Coordinate with Photographer:
  • Phocus.io issues invitations to photographers in area.
  • Phocus.io confirms with Client, the Date, Time and Shoot details.

The Photo Shoot

Photo Shoot:
  • Photographer will do photographs based on package & selected options.

Review & Select Photos:
  • Low resolution preview copies of photos will be on-line at Phocus.io site.
  • Client will select photos for final copies.

Final delivery:
  • Selected photos will be post-processed, and available for download.

The Photo Shoot

Completion

Feedback & Ratings:
  • Client can provide comments & ratings on Photographer and on completed photos.
  • You can tell us if you had a good experience, with good results – or, if you didn’t.

Approval & Payment:
  • Photographer will be paid after client receives photos, and when client payment is confirmed.
  • Satisfaction with results: If client doesn’t believe deliverables meet their expectations, Phocus.io will manage a resolution process with the photographer.
