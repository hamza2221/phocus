You maintain sole discretion to bid on a work order, and to reject any work order if you deem the work unprofitable, or for any other reason. C. Phocus expects that as an independent contractor, you have other sources of business and Phocus has no obligation to provide you with an opportunity to bid on a minimum number of work orders.
